# Akmens darbai Meta Ad Spec

## Goal

Create a cleaner, higher-converting vertical Meta ad based on the Canva concept `4 klaidos renkantis paminklą`.

## Format

- Size: `1080 x 1920`
- Use case: Meta Story / Reel / vertical placement
- Output asset: `akmens-darbai-4-klaidos-meta-story.png`

## Design direction

- Keep cemetery and monument imagery as the emotional base.
- Reduce the number of overlapping blocks.
- Use one warm beige headline container and dark charcoal information cards.
- Preserve a premium, respectful, informative tone.
- Keep all key content inside mobile-safe central margins.

## Hierarchy

1. Headline
2. Four mistake cards
3. Contact CTA

## Key corrections from the Canva draft

- Simplified the top headline area into one dominant block.
- Removed the heavy pile-up of overlapping rounded panels.
- Made the numbering system consistent across all four mistakes.
- Shortened and normalized the line lengths for faster reading.
- Created a stable top-to-bottom reading flow.
- Pulled the CTA into a separate closing panel instead of letting it compete with body copy.
